This CLI needs the following:
-- setmax: 
    this will take reps x weight, calculate a 1RM then multiply it x 0.9 to get training max

-- incmax: add 5lbs to the max at the end of each cycle.

-- log: 
    take lift + weight + reps and logs this

-- showlog:
     display the 531 log. default might be past month
     flag could get all logs for a lift
